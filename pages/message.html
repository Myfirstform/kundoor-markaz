Message from the Principal

بسم الله الرحمان الرحيم

It is with great honor and gratitude that I extend a warm welcome to you all on behalf of Kundoor Markazu Ssaqafathil Islamiyya. Our institution stands as a beacon of knowledge, faith, and academic excellence, dedicated to shaping the next generation of Islamic scholars, educators, and professionals.

At Kundoor Markazu Ssaqafathil Islamiyya, we believe that education is not just about acquiring knowledge but about developing character, wisdom, and a deep sense of responsibility toward society. Our unique approach integrates Islamic teachings with modern education, ensuring that our students are well-prepared to navigate the challenges of today's world while staying true to their Islamic values and moral principles.

Since its inception, our college has been committed to providing an environment where students can grow intellectually, spiritually, and socially. We emphasize academic rigor, ethical discipline, and leadership development, nurturing individuals who will serve as role models and contributors to the Ummah and humanity at large.

Our well-structured programs, including Islamic Graduation (Faizy & Ashkary), Higher Secondary (Plus Two), and Undergraduate (UG) courses, offer a comprehensive curriculum that blends traditional Islamic sciences with contemporary subjects. Through experienced faculty, modern facilities, and a disciplined learning atmosphere, we strive to equip our students with the skills, knowledge, and spiritual strength to excel in both religious and worldly pursuits.

I encourage our students to approach their education with passion, dedication, and sincerity, seeking not only to gain knowledge but to implement it for the benefit of themselves and society. Education is a lifelong journey, and it is through continuous learning, discipline, and devotion that one attains true success in this world and the Hereafter.

To our esteemed parents and guardians, I extend my heartfelt appreciation for entrusting us with the responsibility of educating and guiding your children. Your support, prayers, and encouragement are invaluable to their success.

May Allah (SWT) bless us all with knowledge that benefits, hearts that seek the truth, and the strength to uphold righteousness in all aspects of life.

With prayers and best wishes,

PK Abdul Gafoor Al Qasimi

Principal, Kundoor Markazu Ssaqafathil Islamiyya, Kundoor

Kundoor Markaz

A premier Islamic educational institution dedicated to excellence in both religious and modern education.
