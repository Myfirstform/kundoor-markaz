About us

At Markazu Ssaqafathi Islamiyya Kundoor, we are committed to nurturing a new generation of scholars who excel in both religious and modern education. As an esteemed Islamic religious college, we provide a synthesized education system that integrates classical Islamic studies with contemporary academic disciplines, ensuring that our students are well-prepared to contribute meaningfully to society.

Our Courses

faizy course

the faizy program offers deep knowledge in Qur'anic studies, Hadith, Fiqh, and Arabic, english, urdu languages. Students begin their studies at here and complete the final two years at Jamia Nooriyya Arabiyya, Pattikkad, gaining advanced scholarly training to become Islamic scholars and educators.

ashkary course

The ashkary program provides comprehensive training in Islamic education. Unlike the Faizy program, students complete their entire course at here, gaining in-depth knowledge to become Islamic scholars and educators.

higher secondary

Our Higher Secondary (Plus Two) program offers a balanced curriculum that integrates Islamic studies with modern education. Students receive a strong foundation in several educational sectors, along with essential Islamic teachings, preparing them for higher education and future careers.

UG program

Our Undergraduate (UG) Program combines Islamic studies with modern academics, offering a well-rounded education. Students gain expertise in Islamic sciences, language, and contemporary subjects, equipping them for higher studies and diverse career opportunities.

welcome to kundoor markaz

At Kundoor Markazu Ssaqafathil Islamiyya, we are committed to nurturing a generation of knowledgeable, ethical, and visionary individuals who excel in both Islamic and modern education. Our institution provides a unique learning environment where students develop intellectual depth, moral integrity, and leadership qualities. Through a balanced curriculum, experienced faculty, and state-of-the-art facilities, we strive to shape scholars who uphold Islamic values while contributing positively to society. Education at our college is not just about gaining knowledge but about building character, fostering discipline, and instilling a lifelong passion for learning. We invite students to embark on this enlightening journey of faith, wisdom, and excellence, preparing them to face the challenges of the modern world while staying firmly rooted in their Islamic identity. May Allah (SWT) bless our efforts and guide us toward success in this world and the Hereafter.

explore our campus

Welcome to Kundoor Markazu Ssaqafathil Islamiyya, a center of excellence where knowledge, spirituality, and modern education come together to create a dynamic learning environment. mission & vision

Mission

To provide a balanced and comprehensive education that seamlessly integrates Islamic knowledge with modern academics. We strive to cultivate intellectual, spiritual, and moral excellence, preparing students to become leaders, scholars, and responsible global citizens.

Vision

To establish an institution that nurtures scholars and professionals who embody Islamic values, academic excellence, and social responsibility. Our vision is to create a generation that can bridge the gap between traditional wisdom and contemporary advancements, contributing meaningfully to society.

Philosophy

We believe that education is a transformative journey that blends faith, knowledge, and character development. Our philosophy is rooted in cultivating wisdom, ethical integrity, and leadership, ensuring that our students are spiritually enriched, intellectually empowered, and socially responsible.
