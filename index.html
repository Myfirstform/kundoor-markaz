Academic Programs

Our Courses

Comprehensive programs designed to provide excellence in Islamic and modern education

Faizy Program

Deep knowledge in Qur'anic studies, Hadith, Fiqh, and Arabic, English, Urdu languages. Students complete final two years at Jamia Nooriyya Arabiyya, Pattikkad.

Ashkary Program

Comprehensive training in Islamic education. Students complete their entire course here, gaining in-depth knowledge to become Islamic scholars and educators.

Higher Secondary

Balanced curriculum integrating Islamic studies with modern education, providing strong foundation in multiple educational sectors along with essential Islamic teachings.

UG Program

Combines Islamic studies with modern academics, offering well-rounded education. Students gain expertise in Islamic sciences, language, and contemporary subjects.

Principal's Address

Welcome to Kundoor Markaz

At Kundoor Markazu Ssaqafathil Islamiyya, we are committed to nurturing a generation of knowledgeable, ethical, and visionary individuals who excel in both Islamic and modern education. Our institution provides a unique learning environment where students develop intellectual depth, moral integrity, and leadership qualities. Through a balanced curriculum, experienced faculty, and state-of-the-art facilities, we strive to shape scholars who uphold Islamic values while contributing positively to society.

Education at our college is not just about gaining knowledge but about building character, fostering discipline, and instilling a lifelong passion for learning. We invite students to embark on this enlightening journey of faith, wisdom, and excellence, preparing them to face the challenges of the modern world while staying firmly rooted in their Islamic identity. May Allah (SWT) bless our efforts and guide us toward success in this world and the Hereafter.

Our Values

Mission & Vision

Mission

To provide a balanced and comprehensive education that seamlessly integrates Islamic knowledge with modern academics. We strive to cultivate intellectual, spiritual, and moral excellence, preparing students to become leaders, scholars, and responsible global citizens.

Vision

To establish an institution that nurtures scholars and professionals who embody Islamic values, academic excellence, and social responsibility. Our vision is to create a generation that can bridge the gap between traditional wisdom and contemporary advancements, contributing meaningfully to society.

Philosophy

We believe that education is a transformative journey that blends faith, knowledge, and character development. Our philosophy is rooted in cultivating wisdom, ethical integrity, and leadership, ensuring that our students are spiritually enriched, intellectually empowered, and socially responsible.
